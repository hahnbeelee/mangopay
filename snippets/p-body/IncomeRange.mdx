<ParamField body="IncomeRange" type="integer" >     
**Allowed values:** 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6

The bracket indicating the income of the user. The brackets are:  
- 1: < 18K  
- 2: 18K - 30K  
- 3: 30K - 50K  
- 4: 50K - 80K  
- 5: 80K - 120K  
- 6: > 120K  

Returned `null` if `UserCategory` is `PAYER`.
