#### Additional information  

Mangopayâ€™s teams may block users for reasons of suspected fraudulent behavior or compliance risk.  

#### Causes  

This error may occur when attempting to make an instant payout to a user whose outflows are blocked.  

#### What to do  

In such cases, the platform can take advantage of the <a href="/api-reference/user-regulatory-status/view-user-regulatory-status">View a User Regulatory Status</a> endpoint to know more about the reasons why the end user is blocked. From there, actions can be taken based on the returned `ActionCode`.
