#### Causes  

This error occurs when attempting to make a refund with a fees `Amount` superior to the platform's Fees Wallet `Balance` amount.  

#### What to do  

In such cases, platforms can:  
- Wait for their Fees Wallet to be credited again in order to make a new attempt for the refund
