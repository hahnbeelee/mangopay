#### Causes  

This error occurs when the end user was blocked by Mangopay’s team because a fraudulent behavior or a compliance risk is suspected.  

#### What to do  

Take advantage of the <a href="/api-reference/user-regulatory-status/view-user-regulatory-status">View a User Regulatory Status</a> endpoint to get the `ActionCode` for the blocked user. From there, you can refer to the Blocked users article to know what to do next.
