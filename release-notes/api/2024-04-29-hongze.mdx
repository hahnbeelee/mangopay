### StatementDescriptor available on SDD refunds  

Platforms can now send the `StatementDescriptor` parameter on refunds of SEPA Direct Debit pay-ins.  

On refunds of other payment methods, as well as transfer refunds, the parameter is ignored.   

The following endpoints have been updated:
